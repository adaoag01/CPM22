Bangus Recipe



Gising-Gising Minced Bangus
Prep time
30 mins
Cook time
30 mins
Total time
1 hr

         SERVES: 7

Ingredients

250g Sarangani Bay Milkfish Fillet (chopped)
2 tbsp cooking oil
1 tsp ginger, chopped
1 tbsp garlic, minced
¼ cup chopped onions
2 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)
2 cups sliced green beans (Baguio beans)
1 cup coconut cream
3 pcs bird’s eye chilies (siling labuyo)

Directions:

In a sauté pan heat cooking oil. Saute ginger, garlic, onions and shrimp paste. Add chopped bangus meat and cook for 5 minutes. Add green beans, the half of coconut cream. Cook for 2 minutes over medium heat.
Lastly add the remaining coconut cream and chilies. Stir once and remove from heat. Serve hot.
